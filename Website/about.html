Our Story

A beachside escape designed for restoration and adventure.

Founded on a quiet stretch of sun-warmed coastline, The Last Resort Hotel began as a humble beach retreat where travelers could reconnect with the sound of the ocean and the beauty of untouched shoreline. Over time, it has grown into a full-service coastal sanctuary known for relaxed luxury, natural elegance, and unforgettable sunsets.

Whether guests come to explore the coast, unwind in the warm sand, or savor fresh ocean-inspired dining, the resort remains committed to blending comfort, nature, and timeless hospitality.
